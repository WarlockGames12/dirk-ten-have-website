Virtual System 3: Revolutions Chapter 1 & 2 (2019-2020)

Description:

Several years went by after Z3R0 returned to the void. The old cast has grown and have their own lives going on. Until an new and improved Rija System has been released. After Glitch has been missing for a few days, they are trying to search for him. Who has him kidnapped, is someone trying to send a message? or is something else at play here?
