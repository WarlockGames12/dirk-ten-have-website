To Hell and Back (2025)

Description:

The Player was send to hell after putting pineapple on pizza, which is the greatest sin with Italian standards. Where you have to face the 9 inner circles of hell to escape and has to face an boss at every level.

This game was created for an school assignment, where I had to work with 3 other guys, I am learning alot about Monogame and quite more about C#, atleast I am not limited anymore with Unity alone.
