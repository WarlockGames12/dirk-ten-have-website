Rocket Dungeon(2021)


Discription:

This is a Space Dungeon Dasher, where you have to avoid the aliens from attacking you and killing you. The point of target is to find the portal of the level.

----------------------------------------------------------------

Before working on this game, we also had decided to make a horror game, but my teammates decided against it, because it would be more difficult working on that. So we went for making a 2D game.

Next to this text is the code what I used for the enemy behaviour, I was quite happy with the result for how the enemy reacted to the player.

We had planned for making more levels, but we didn't went through with it, because we wanted to work on something else. I even fixed a bug I didn't even know how I fixed it, which was quite funny.

On further note, I had no problem and no particular errors we were concerned over, because we would know how to deal with those issues at the time when making that game.

----------------------------------------------------------------

This game was made for the 3rd period and was the first game I made with other people inside of Unity. Which was fun, to be honest. But I had to put my teammates to work, because they didn't do much before, so I said that we had to do something and we finished this game not long soon after.
