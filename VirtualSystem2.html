Virtual System 2: Collision Of Worlds (2019)

Description:

Z3R0 woke up inside of the void after a year. A voice spoke to him that a collision with the void and the normal world are going to collide. Which can cause the collapse of the whole Rija System.

Enter the world once more inside of the Rija System en defeat the villainus Team Rage and discover new things that weren't revealed in the first game of Virtual System!
