Virtual System 2: Collision of Worlds (2019)


Description:

Z3R0 woke up inside of the void after a year. A voice spoke to him that a collision with the void and the normal world are going to collide. Which can cause the collapse of the whole Rija System.

Enter the world once more inside of the Rija System en defeat the villainus Team Rage and discover new things that weren't revealed in the first game of Virtual System!

----------------------------------------------------------------

I even recieved some help for art to make this game from Kumulus Comics, they drew the characters themselves to make it more fun. which was really cool to be honest and I was really hoping to work with them again.

While I was doing the last few things for the game itself and started to export the game, for some reason it had an error what I could not solve and find, I delayed the release of this game to fix this bug. Later I found the bug and the bug was that one of the characters in one city, was corrupted, so that means I have to remove the character, that was it. I just felt stupid after that simple bug fix, but later I realised, it was very common.
